About Quality Cable

Q CABLE is a co-brand of Quality Cable Industries Limited. It’s a cable manufacturing company based in Bangladesh and one of the leading manufacturers of House Wiring Cables & Industrial Cables in Bangladesh. Which is Established in 2013. Since 2013 to till now Q Cable specializes in the manufacture of Domestic cables, Power cables, Control cables, Telecommunication and Submersible cables, and Overhead conductors for domestic markets. Quality Cable Industries Ltd is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. Manufacturing within its own plants and obtaining all necessary certifications from BSTI, BUET, and MIST by implementing stringent quality control processes and providing its customers with a guarantee of product, ensuring wires quality, excellence, and reliability.
